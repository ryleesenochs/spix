Spix Macaws Return to the Wild!

The Spix Macaw, once thought to be extinct in the wild, has made a remarkable comeback.

  • Breeding programs were successful
  • Their natural habitats were restored

Conservation Efforts and Success Stories

  • Conservation efforts have focused on habitat restoration and protection.

Thanks to dedicated conservationists and organizations, the Spix Macaw population is increasing

Reintroduction

In 2020, the first group of captive-bred Spix Macaws were released into the wild.
